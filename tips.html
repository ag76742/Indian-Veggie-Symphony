Popular Vegetarian Indian Dishes

Dish Name Description Ingredients
Paneer Butter Masala A rich and creamy paneer (Indian cottage cheese) dish cooked in a tomato-based sauce. Paneer, tomatoes, cream, spices
Chana Masala Spicy and tangy chickpea curry, flavored with aromatic Indian spices. Chickpeas, tomatoes, onions, spices
Aloo Gobi A classic North Indian dish featuring potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked with spices. Potatoes, cauliflower, turmeric, cumin seeds
Malai Kofta Aromatic and creamy dumplings in a rich tomato-based gravy. Potatoes, paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cashews, cream, tomatoes, spices.
Veg Biryani Fragrant rice layered with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices. Basmati rice, mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), spices, saffron, nuts.
Idli Vada Soft steamed rice cakes and crispy lentil doughnuts, served with chutney and sambar. Idli batter (fermented rice and lentil batter), urad dal (black gram), rice, chutney, sambar.
Dosa Thin, crispy pancakes made from fermented rice and lentil batter, served with savory filling and chutney. Dosa batter, potatoes, onions, spices, chutney (coconut or tomato).
